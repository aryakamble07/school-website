About Us

"Where Learning Begins, Dreams Take Flight."

Shri Brahmendra Swami High School, Dhavdashi, nestled amidst the scenic Meruling hills, stands as a beacon of quality education and rich values in rural Maharashtra.
Established in 1983 through the vision of dedicated local leaders, the school has grown from humble beginnings to a modern institution with state-of-the-art facilities, including smart classrooms, digital learning tools, and a fully solar-powered campus. With a strong focus on academics, vocational training, sports, and cultural development, the school nurtures well-rounded individuals who go on to serve the nation with pride. Parents seeking a holistic and future-ready education for their children will find Shri Brahmendra Swami High School a truly inspiring choice.
