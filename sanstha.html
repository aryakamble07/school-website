Shri Brahmendra Swami Sevamandal Dhawadshi

Dhavdashi is a remote village in Satara district from Maharashtra state in India. The village is totally dependent on agriculture with no sources of other income for villagers. Agriculture is also dependent only on seasonal rainwater with no irrigation facilities. Therefore, by and large, the villagers are very poor. Brahmendraswami High School was established in 1983 on this background to provide school education to the needy and poor village students. Right from the beginning, the education in this school was imparted free of cost and that motto still remains very much intact. The school gives education from 5th to 10th standard. The school has been helped by many benevolent donors across society. With the help of these donations, the school has its own two buildings. The school has its own playground. The school is run by a charitable trust 'Brahmendraswami Seva Mandal'. The school imparts free education to the village students and the students from adjoining other villages. The school has 5th to 10th classrooms.
